CAMP PARAISO

Camp Paraiso is a Bali-inspired resort in Bongabon, Nueva Ecija, known as the "Bali of Nueva Ecija" for its scenic and tranquil atmosphere. It features numerous Instagrammable spots like a love nest and waterfalls, a man-made pool with cold spring water, and offers activities such as mountain climbing, river trekking, swimming, and glamping. Accommodations range from cabins to villas, and the resort also provides options like boodle fights and karaoke.
